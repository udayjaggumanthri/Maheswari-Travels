Araku Sightseeing

Must-Visit Attractions in Araku

Top Attraction
Borra Caves

Deep inside Ananthagiri hills, these caves house beautiful formations formed by Katiki river action over millions of years. Beautifully lit up for tourists.

Nature Cascade
Katiki Waterfalls

Fed by the Gosthani River, this waterfall drops 100 feet. Requires a short jeep drive and pleasant forest trek, perfect for nature lovers.

Botanical
Padmapuram Gardens

Originally built to supply food for soldiers in World War II, it's now a beautiful park with rare plants, tree houses, and a miniature toy train.

Scenic View
Galikonda Viewpoint

The highest viewpoint in Visakhapatnam district, offering panoramic views of deep valleys, green hills, railways, and misty ghat roads.
